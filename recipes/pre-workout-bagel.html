Pre Workout Bagel

It is a bagel that you can eat preworkout that can give you the quick carbs that you will need for a terrific pump.

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon Swirl Bagel
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Banana
  • Ground Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Toast the bagel
  2. Spread the peanut butter on top
  3. Cut banana into slices and add to the top
  4. Add honey on top of the banana
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon
