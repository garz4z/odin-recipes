Chicken Tacos

These are chicken tacos that can be made quickly and are high in protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chicken
  • 2 carb balance tortillas
  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup of salsa of choice
  • Garlic Powder
  • Garlic Salt
  • Spice of choice(if desired)

Steps

  1. Warm up your rice in the microwave for 30 seconds.
  2. Drain your chicken and add to bowl; warm in microwave for 1 minute.
  3. Add salsa, garlic powder, garlic salt and spice of choice and mix.
  4. Fill tortilal to liking and enjoy!
